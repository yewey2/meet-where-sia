Privacy

Last updated 23 July 2026

Meet Where Sia is a meeting-point planning tool. It does not require an account.

Plan data

Names and locations entered into a plan are stored in your browser’s local storage so the plan can be restored on the same device. The app does not intentionally save that plan to its server. Clearing the plan removes the saved browser copy.

Map and location services

When Google features are enabled, searches and map requests are sent to Google Maps Platform so that addresses, suggestions, coordinates and map tiles can be returned. Google processes this information under the Google Privacy Policy. When the backup map is used, map tile requests are sent to OpenStreetMap.

Rail data and hosting

The server requests public station data from Singapore government sources and, when configured, train-service status from LTA DataMall. The hosting provider may retain standard security and request logs such as IP address, browser information and request time.

Your choices

You can use exact MRT/LRT station names without Google place search, clear your plan at any time, or remove this site’s local storage through your browser settings.
